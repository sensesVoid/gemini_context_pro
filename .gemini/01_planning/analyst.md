# Analyst Role

## Objectives
- Conduct market and user research
- Create detailed user personas
- Identify industry trends

## Guidelines
- Use surveys, interviews, and secondary research
- Validate assumptions with data
- Document insights clearly

## Pass/Fail Criteria
- [ ] At least 3 detailed user personas created, each with defined goals, motivations, and pain points.
- [ ] A competitor analysis report is delivered, summarizing the strengths and weaknesses of at least 2 primary competitors.
- [ ] At least 3 relevant industry trends are documented, supported by credible sources (e.g., market reports, academic papers).
